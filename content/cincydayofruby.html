What is Cincinnati Day of Ruby?

Ruby is becoming the language of choice for Agile companies that want to deliver functionality before their customers need it. Ruby on Rails is the web framework that supports some of the highest volume sites on the internet.

You need to be up to speed on this technology today, so that when it rolls into your company, you can be the leader.

This one day session takes you through the basics of Ruby the language in the morning and then how to make something real in Ruby on Rails in the afternoon. Get your hands on the future while surrounded by the experts to help you along.
